About me

Pak Lun Fung received the Ph.D. degree in atmospheric science from the Institute for Atmospheric and Earth System Research, University of Helsinki, Helsinki, Finland, in 2022.,His dissertation focuses on the proxy derivation of air quality parameters in urban regions. His current research topics include microscopic traffic emission modeling and geospatial analysis on environmental data.
